Our Work as Consultants

Aerospace Company

Increate Software provided Engineering Management support to help an major Aerospace vendor interface with a primer offshore development company. The mission was to develop a data distribution system to manage the complex streams of data that needed to be processed and delivered to the Aerospace companies' clients. The Aerospace company was experienced in providing vendor management and had a mature software development process and therefore needed the most support with ensuring good communication between upper / executive management of both companies.


Health Care Company

Increate Software embedded on-site at a Health Care company in San Jose, CA. The company had contractually obligated release in the three month time horizon that was mission critical. Increate software provided support reviewing requirements, detailing scope of engagement, planning effort, tracking progress to goals, evaluating priorities, and providing feedback to customer. With this support the project was completed within the contractually obligated period.
